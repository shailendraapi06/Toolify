Frequently Asked Questions

Will it create extra PDFs?

No. Our tool follows your rules exactly. If you define 2 ranges, you will get exactly 2 PDFs. If you extract specific pages, they will all be combined into just 1 PDF.

How do I download multiple files?

If your split results in more than one PDF file, we automatically bundle them into a single ZIP archive for a convenient one-click download.

Is my original PDF changed?

Not at all. Your original file remains untouched on your computer. We simply create new, smaller PDFs based on the pages you select.
