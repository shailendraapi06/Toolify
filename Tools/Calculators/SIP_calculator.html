What is SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method provided by mutual funds to help investors invest in a disciplined manner. SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals in your selected mutual fund scheme.

How SIP Calculator Works

The SIP calculator works on the principle of compound interest. It uses the formula: FV = P √ó [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] √ó (1 + r). Here, P is the monthly investment, r is the monthly rate of return, and n is the total number of installments.

Benefits of Using a SIP Calculator

Using an online SIP calculator helps you visualize your financial future. It allows you to adjust variables like investment amount or tenure to see how it impacts your final maturity value. This clarity helps in setting realistic financial goals for retirement, education, or wealth building.
