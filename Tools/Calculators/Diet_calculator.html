What is a Diet & Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator is a tool that estimates how many calories you burn each day (TDEE) and how many you should consume to reach a specific fitness goal. It considers your biological factors like age, weight, and height, alongside your lifestyle habits.

How Daily Calories are Calculated

Our tool uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation to find your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)‚Äîthe calories you burn at rest. We then multiply this by an activity factor to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Adjustments are then made to create a calorie deficit or surplus based on your goal.

Calories for Weight Loss and Muscle Gain

To lose weight, you generally need a calorie deficit (consuming fewer calories than you burn). For muscle gain (bulking), a slight calorie surplus combined with weight training is necessary. Consistency is more important than perfection‚Äîalways aim for sustainable changes.
