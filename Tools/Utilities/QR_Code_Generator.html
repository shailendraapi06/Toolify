Why Use Professional QR Codes?

QR codes (Quick Response codes) have become a standard in the digital-physical bridge. Whether you're sharing a business card (vCard), setting up an automated payment gateway (UPI), or simplifying guest WiFi access, a well-formatted QR code ensures a 100% scan success rate.

Technical Specifications

QRPro Studio uses the ISO/IEC 18004 standard. We support all four levels of Reed-Solomon Error Correction, allowing your QR codes to remain scanable even if they are partially obscured or damaged. Our WiFi formatting follows the standard WIFI:S:ssid;T:type;P:password;; protocol compatible with both iOS and Android.
